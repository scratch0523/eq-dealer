About Us

About our business Firm

Equipride Ltd specialises in quality equestrian products with both the horse and rider in mind. Based in Leeds, UK, we supply customers around the world. We understand and appreciate the relationship between a horse and a rider and at Equipride we carry that understanding through in our approach and business vision: “everything we create and design is to be loved by the horse and the rider”

Our employees bring with them a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the equine industry . And to utilise these effectively, our overall approach is based on a distinct set of values which we believe stand us apart:

A focus on quality – our leather products are supple, our rugs are waterproof, our products create an impact.

A focus on the customer – from the design and manufacturing to the supply, the customer is at the heart of our operations.

A focus on the industry – we work closely with local riding schools, attend national and international events and trade shows. We have attended the British Equestrian Trade Association (BETA International) since 2005. Visit us at our annual trade stands at the Royal Welsh Show and Your Horse Live.

A focus on giving back – we donate supplies and a percentage of our proﬁts to local charities. Look out for products with charity tags where sales translate to direct donations to the nominated charity. In addition, we sponsor local riding schools and local shows.
